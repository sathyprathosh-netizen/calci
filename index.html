Intern's Income Calculator

This calculator helps interns estimate their monthly stipend and incentives based on the revenue they generate during their internship period.

Users need to provide the following details:

  • Intern Name
  • Designation
  • Internship Month (1–6)
  • Current Month Revenue
  • Previous Month Revenue (if any)

The base stipend of ₹8,000 is awarded only when the intern meets the required revenue milestone for the selected month using current month revenue.

The required revenue targets are:

  • Month 1–3: ₹40,000
  • Month 4–5: ₹60,000
  • Month 6: ₹80,000

These milestones determine eligibility for the base stipend.

If your current month revenue exceeds the required milestone, you will receive a 10% incentive on the extra revenue generated, in addition to the base stipend.

If the milestone is not achieved, the base stipend will not be granted. However, you will still receive a 10% incentive on the revenue generated.

Internship Revenue Guidelines

Month 1-3 Milestone: ₹40,000
Month 4-5 Milestone: ₹60,000
Month 6 Milestone: ₹80,000
  • Base Stipend (₹8,000): Awarded ONLY if the Current Month revenue meets or exceeds the milestone. Exact milestone hits receive only the base stipend.
  • Incentive on Current Revenue: 10% bonus is awarded only on the revenue that exceeds the required milestone. If the milestone is not met, a 10% incentive is awarded on the entire current month revenue.
  • Pending/Previous Revenue: Previous month (pending) revenue always grants an unconditional 10% incentive, but does not count toward unlocking the base stipend.
  • Zero Current Revenue: If no revenue is generated in the current month, no stipend is given, but any pending revenue still earns a 10% incentive.
